Conquest of Shadows

Build your stronghold. Command your armies. Dominate a living world.

Enter a vast hex-based Overworld where every territory can be conquered. Construct your castle, expand your base, gather resources, and defend your lands from rival players. Every decision matters as you rise to power in a world shaped entirely by players.

Build & Evolve Your Empire

  • Construct key buildings such as Barracks, Archery Ranges, Iron Mines, Lumber Camps, Stone Quarries, and more
  • Upgrade your base to boost production and accelerate your rise
  • Strengthen your castle walls and secure your realm from invaders

Conquer the Overworld

  • Explore a massive 100×100 strategic map
  • Claim new territories to expand your influence and unlock powerful bonuses
  • Launch real-time attacks with travel timers and dynamic battle outcomes
  • Defend your land using army missions and strategic positioning

Command Powerful Armies

  • Train diverse units and build custom battle compositions
  • Calculate attack power, defense strength, and win probabilities with server-validated combat
  • Send armies to attack, capture, defend, or abandon territories with animated travel lines and countdown timers

Gather Resources & Grow Stronger

  • Mine gold, iron, lumber, and stone from both your base and conquered lands
  • Boost your hourly income through expansion and building synergy
  • Spend resources to construct, upgrade, and fuel your war machine

Discover Dungeons & Ancient Relics

  • Spend Adventuring Points to delve into dangerous dungeons
  • Battle unique creatures, earn experience, level up, and unlock rewards
  • Collect relic fragments to unlock powerful bonuses

Become a Legend

Whether you prefer rapid expansion, fortress-style defense, dungeon diving, or strategic warfare, Conquest of Shadows offers endless ways to rise. Claim your first territory, build your empire, unlock magical power, and carve your name into a world that lives and reacts around you.
