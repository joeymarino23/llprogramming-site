Supernatural Slayer

Gather weapons, equipment and potions to battle werewolves, trolls, vampires and more!

Increase in level as you defeat supernatural creatures in one on one combat. You are an adventurer learning the ways of the Order of Supernatural Slayers, but recently creatures in the region have been acting strangely, working together to cause difficulty for the Order. It is up to you to work with the Order to get to the root of the problem and maintain peace in the region.

Character Creation

  • Take your chances rolling stats for your character for each of the 6 attributes
  • Scores of 9-12 are average; lower will result in penalties while higher will result in bonuses
  • Or start with a premade character that has no penalties in any area, but no bonuses either!

In-Game Currency

  • Gather gold to purchase potions, armor, weapons, rings and amulets
  • Outfit your character and increase your ability to survive in a hostile world

Inventory

  • After obtaining or purchasing new items, head to your inventory to equip them
  • Use the sell screen to obtain gold for additional items and more at the shop

Questboard

  • Visit the quest board to see what the Order of Supernatural Slayers is having difficulty with
  • Lend a helping hand and be rewarded with gold and items for completing quests

Battle

  • Attack creatures, but beware some are much more dangerous than others
  • If your health gets low or you encounter a fearsome creature, use the retreat button!
  • Health potions can be used in battle after they are obtained

Healing

  • The Order has a healer on site after battle to repair damage inflicted by creatures

Game Center

  • Compete with your friends to obtain achievements and high scores
  • See who can advance their character to the highest level
  • Issue challenges to compete in Game Center
