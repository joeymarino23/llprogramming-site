Privacy Policy – Mermaid Maths

Effective Date: January 12th, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Mermaid Maths ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by L&L Programming. The Application is an educational game designed to help children practice addition.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Mermaid Maths is designed with privacy in mind. The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its users.

What information does the Application collect?

None. Mermaid Maths does not collect any personal information or data from users. The Application:

  • Does not require registration or account creation
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Does not collect device information or unique identifiers
  • Does not track location data
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does not collect usage analytics

In-App Purchases

Mermaid Maths does not contain any in-app purchases. The Application is a complete product with no additional paid content.

Advertising

Mermaid Maths does not display any advertisements. There are no third-party advertising networks or ad-related data collection within the Application.

Third-Party Services

Mermaid Maths does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect user data. The Application functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to operate.

Children's Privacy

Mermaid Maths is designed for children and is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, including children, there is no risk of children's data being collected, stored, or shared.

Data Storage

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to any external servers and remains entirely under your control. Uninstalling the Application will remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
