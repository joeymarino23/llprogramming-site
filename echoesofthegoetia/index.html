Echoes of the Goetia

Turn-Based PvP RPG

Battle to gain XP, level up, allocate stats, and collect randomized loot.

Echoes of the Goetia is an online multiplayer turn-based RPG where players battle to gain experience, level up their characters, allocate stat points across Strength, Agility, Vitality, Intelligence, and Wisdom, and collect randomized loot including weapons, armor, accessories, and consumables.

Character Progression

  • Gain XP from PvP battles and level up your character
  • Allocate stat points across five attributes: Strength, Agility, Vitality, Intelligence, and Wisdom
  • Track your power score, gold balance, and win/loss record
  • Maintain daily login streaks to earn rewards

Equipment & Loot

  • Collect randomized weapons, armor, accessories, and consumables
  • Equip gear to boost your stats and power score
  • Manage your inventory and optimize your loadout

PvP Combat

  • Engage in turn-based battles against other players online
  • Matchmaking based on your rating (MMR) for fair fights
  • Climb the leaderboards and build your win streak
  • Server-authoritative architecture ensures fair gameplay

Game Center Integration

  • Unlock achievements for battle victories, level milestones, and loot collection
  • Compete on leaderboards for player level, power score, and PvP rating
